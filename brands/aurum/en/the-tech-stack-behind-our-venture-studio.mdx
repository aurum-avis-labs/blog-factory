Choosing the right tech stack is one of the most consequential decisions in early-stage product development. At Aurum Avis Labs, we've refined our stack to optimize for speed, maintainability, and scalability.

## Our Core Principles

Before diving into specific technologies, here are the principles that guide our choices:

- **Speed without debt** — We move fast, but we don't create technical debt that slows us down later
- **Proven over trendy** — We choose battle-tested tools over the latest hype
- **Cloud-native** — Everything is designed for cloud deployment from day one
- **Developer experience** — Happy developers build better products

## Frontend

For web applications, we typically reach for:

- **Astro** for content-heavy sites and landing pages
- **React** with TypeScript for interactive applications
- **Tailwind CSS** for rapid, consistent styling
- **shadcn/ui** for accessible, customizable UI components

## Backend

Our backend choices depend on the project, but common picks include:

- **Node.js** or **Python** depending on the domain
- **PostgreSQL** as our primary database
- RESTful APIs or GraphQL depending on client needs

## Infrastructure

We standardize on **Microsoft Azure** for cloud infrastructure:

- Azure App Service or Azure Static Web Apps for hosting
- Azure DevOps or GitHub Actions for CI/CD
- Application Insights for monitoring and analytics
- Infrastructure as Code for reproducible environments

## Why This Matters for Clients

When you work with us, you're not inheriting a fragile prototype. You're getting a production-ready foundation that your future engineering team can build on confidently.

Every technology choice we make is documented, and we provide thorough handover materials so your team can hit the ground running.
