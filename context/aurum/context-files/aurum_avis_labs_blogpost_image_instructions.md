# Aurum Avis Labs — Blogpost Supporting Image Instructions

Create premium supporting images for Aurum Avis Labs blogposts with a refined, institutional venture-studio aesthetic.

## Core Style
- Use a deep black or near-black background as the foundation.
- Apply rich gold accents sparingly for emphasis, highlights, glows, lines, or focal details.
- Maintain a clean, minimal, high-end composition with strong visual hierarchy.
- The overall look should feel premium, strategic, futuristic, and credible — never playful, cartoonish, or stock-photo-like.

## Visual Direction
- Focus on abstract business, AI, venture building, product strategy, validation, automation, and innovation themes.
- Prefer elegant UI-inspired elements, subtle system diagrams, structured workflows, data layers, abstract geometric forms, or high-end digital environments.
- Use soft depth, restrained lighting, and polished contrast rather than loud effects.
- Images should support the article topic without becoming cluttered or overly literal.

## Color System
- Background base: `#000000`
- Secondary dark surfaces: `#0A0A0A` and `#111111`
- Primary gold accent: `#D4AF37`
- Deep gold highlight: `#C99700`
- Soft gold glow / secondary accent: `#F2D16B`
- Primary text on dark: `#F5F5F5`
- Secondary text: `#B3B3B3`
- Muted text / lines: `#6B6B6B`
- Supporting neutral: `#D9D9D9`
- Gold should be used as a premium highlight, not as a full-frame dominant color.

## Composition Rules
- Keep the image centered around one clear visual idea.
- Leave enough negative space so headings or blog layout overlays remain readable.
- Avoid busy scenes, excessive tiny details, or random decorative elements.
- Favor balance, symmetry, and controlled motion.

## Brand Fit
- The image should feel aligned with Aurum Avis Labs as a Swiss AI venture studio: precise, modern, ambitious, and trustworthy.
- Visual tone should communicate execution quality, strategic clarity, and technical sophistication.

## Avoid
- Bright colorful palettes.
- Cheap-looking gold gradients.
- Playful mascots or cartoon scenes.
- Generic handshake/business stock imagery.
- Overloaded dashboards or noisy compositions.
- Flat low-effort SaaS clipart aesthetics.

## Output Goal
Each image should look like premium editorial artwork for a high-end technology and venture brand — polished enough to sit on a black-and-gold landing page and visually reinforce authority, innovation, and build quality.
