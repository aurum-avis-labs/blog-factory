KitchenCrew is an AI-powered hospitality management platform that helps restaurants run inventory, ordering, menu planning, and operational analysis in one connected system. It is designed to replace fragmented manual workflows such as spreadsheets, paper-based tracking, and disconnected supplier coordination with a more efficient, data-driven process.  ￼

Ideal Target Audience

KitchenCrew is built for:
	•	restaurant owners
	•	chefs and kitchen managers
	•	multi-location restaurant groups
	•	hospitality operators focused on efficiency, cost control, and reducing food waste

It is especially relevant for businesses that want better visibility into stock, purchasing, and menu decisions.  ￼

Core Features

KitchenCrew includes:
	•	inventory and expiry tracking
	•	automated ordering and supplier coordination
	•	chat-based stock and order interaction
	•	smart menu planning based on stock and seasonality
	•	accounting and document preparation support
	•	dashboards for costs, losses, supplier performance, and operational trends  ￼
