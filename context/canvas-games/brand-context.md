Inklets (inklets.app) is a mobile multiplayer climbing game made by a small indie studio in Zurich, Switzerland (Aurum Avis Labs GmbH), planned for iOS and Android in Summer 2026. The brand id in this repo is `canvas-games` (the landing page repo is `canvas-games-landing-page`). Blog language: English only.

The game: three players climb the same wobbly, hand-drawn sketchbook page at the same time. Platforms slide, stick, spike, or snap; ink smudges, page monsters, and chaos events knock climbers down; black ink rises from below and swallows the page. The last Inklet standing wins. The core loop is jump, dodge, grab coins, outlast. Tagline: "A three-player climb. Last one standing wins." / "Create your Inklet. Race players online. Don't let the ink catch you."

Key features to reference accurately:
- AI Inklet Generator: players describe a small character in a short text prompt (about 80 characters) and the game turns it into a playable hand-drawn climber they can equip and race with.
- The Sketchbook: the player's collection of climbers, skins, backgrounds (pages), platform styles, trinkets, monsters, stamps, stickers, and rare Inklets. Coins earned in runs become unlocks.
- Characters/skins seen on the landing page: Voidling (base character), Barrel Courier, Sleepy Paper Bat, Box Kid, Bored Ape, Green Forestling, plus AI-made oddballs.
- Waitlist perk: the first 100 confirmed waitlist signups get a launch-only collectible skin that will never be drawn again.
- Launch: Summer 2026 on iOS and Android, free with a pre-order/waitlist phase. Mode at launch: competitive last-player-standing climbing.

Audience and SEO positioning: casual and mid-core mobile players who search for things like "doodle jump", "games like doodle jump", "doodle jump multiplayer", "jumping games", "endless jumper", and "games where you create your own character". Inklets is the multiplayer, hand-drawn, AI-personalized take on the Doodle Jump genre.
