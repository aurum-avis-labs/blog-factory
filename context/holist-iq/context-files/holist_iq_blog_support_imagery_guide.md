# Holist-IQ Blog Support Imagery Guide

Use clean, analytical support imagery that visualizes complexity, systems, decision-making, and cause-and-effect relationships. Preferred image types are abstract system maps, interconnected nodes, feedback loops, strategic dashboards, structured data overlays, and minimal conceptual business scenes. Avoid stock-photo aesthetics, cartoon styles, and overly decorative visuals.

Use a dark background as the default base:  
`#060608`, `#0B0B0F`, `#0E0E13`, `#12121A`

Primary brand accents:  
`#52C46B` for green highlights  
`#6344F9`, `#8720EB`, `#B319DA` for violet/purple accents

Supporting neutrals:  
`#FFFFFF`, `#F5F6FA`, `#D7DBEA`, `#AEB6D6`

The overall look should feel premium, modern, and intelligent. Imagery should communicate clarity through structure: turning messy complexity into understandable systems. Keep compositions minimal, focused, and high-end, with subtle glow, controlled depth, and a precise visual hierarchy.
